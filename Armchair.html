ARMCAHIR

Arm chairs in interior design are pieces of furniture that have both a backrest and armrests. They can be made from a variety of materials, including wood, metal, and upholstered fabric. Arm chairs can be used in both residential and commercial settings. In the home, they are often used in living rooms, dens, and bedrooms as a comfortable places to sit and relax. In businesses, arm chairs are commonly used in waiting areas, conference rooms, and offices.
